# Revfore Flex :: Unleash the Planning Power of OneStream

**Revfore Flex (SV200)** is compatible with OneStream v9.1 and later.

---

## Overview

Revfore Flex enables **decentralized, detailed financial and operational planning** across products, services, assets, employees, and more.

Users can plan in **Excel, the Windows application, or a modern browser**, while maintaining full governance within OneStream.

Built to *flex* with the business, the solution adapts to evolving structures, drivers, and assumptions with **minimal rework**.

## Key Outcomes

- **Accelerate & Adapt** — Speed up planning cycles and adjust quickly as business requirements evolve  
- **Capture & Capitalize** — Manage detailed operational data that drives deeper insight and better decisions  
- **Empower & Extend** — Enable more users to participate in planning through governed, flexible workflows

## Key Capabilities

- Enable **governed planning workflows** with seamless writeback to OneStream  
- Govern **decentralized planning** with flexible workflow profiles and plan assignments  
- Plan at **true operational detail** across products, services, assets, employees, and more
